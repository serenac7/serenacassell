Available Products

Richardson Esthetics hand blends bath, body and inhalation products using 100% natural ingredients and therapeutic grade essential oils for overall body and mind wellness. Pre blended products are available for a variety of uses and treatments. Custom blends can also be created according to specific clientâ€™s treatments and needs.

Soy candle
Made with pure soy wax and essential oils. These candles burn for 40+ hours and can also be used as massage or body oil. Pre-done blends are changed seasonally or by client request.

Bath Salts
Made with Epsom salts, Dead Sea salts, baking soda and essential oils. A bath with these salts will relieve deep muscle tension, relax the mind and leave your skin feeling silky soft. Pre-done blends include sweet relief and sleepy time.

Bath Bombs
Made with all natural ingredients which include Epsom salts, baking soda and essential oils. A bath can be fun and luxurious with these bath bombs. You will get deep relaxation and silky skin from this product.

Whipped Body Butter
Made with pure coconut oil, pure cocoa butter, pure sweet almond or shea butter, sweet almond oil and essential oils. This luxurious body butter will melt into your skin leaving it soft and moisturized. This product can be used on face or body.

Body Oil
Made with coconut, sweet almond, or grapeseed oil and essential oils. Pre-done blends include stress relief, calm (for anxiety), wellness (for treatment and prevention of cold and flu), sweet relief (for muscle tension) and sweet dreams. These oils can be used as body moisturizer, massage oil, or spot treatment. You will receive treatment benefits from these oils as well as soft moisturized skin.

Nose inhaler
Made with essential oils. Pre-done blends include breathe easy (for sinus pain and congestion), and headache healer.

Lip Balm
Made with pure coconut oil, beeswax and natural flavor.

Diffuser Blends
Blended for use in electric diffusers. Pre-done blends include stress relief, calm (for anxiety), wellness (for treatment and prevention of cold and flu), and sweet dreams.

Diffusers for home and car are available for purchase and can be used with diffuser blends.
