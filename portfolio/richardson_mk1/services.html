Indian Head Massage

This course can be used to incorporate a holistic service into your business or for personal use at home. Indian Head Massage is a Holistic Aromatherapy Massage of the upper back, shoulders, neck, and scalp which involves absorption of therapeutic grade essential oils through both massage and inhalation. These treatments have physical, psychological, and emotional benefits.
Participants can practice on one another for the practical component of this program. As a professional upgrade additional models will be required.

Topics:
- Aromatherapy
- Massage
- Client Profiling
- Safety & Contraindications
- Techniques for Service
