Inclement Weather Policy

If buses are cancelled - After The Bell is open. Our van will not be running.

If schools are closed before they open for the day - After The Bell is open.

If schools notify that they will be closing part way through the day - After The Bell is open.

If schools are closed part way through the day - After The Bell is closed. You will have to make arrangements to pick up your children.
