Welcome to Fetch Inc.

Welcome To Fetch Inc., the canine daycare and education facility in Burnside, Dartmouth. We offer lots of experience in Doggie Daycare, Puppy Socialization and Training, Family Obedience, Agility and Rally‑O. Along with daycare services we offer many types and levels of classes and events such as seminars, obedience, agility, run thrus, and fun matches for Conformation, Obedience and Agility. We also rent out space for training, matches, meetings and seminars.

Daycare

A tired dog is a happy dog!

More Details...

Daycare provides your dog with the ideal place to romp and play -- even when you can't be with him! While you toil away at the office, your dog can have a great time making friends in a safe, stimulating environment under the watchful eye of our caring and attentive staff. Play times are always well-supervised and our staff are careful to provide individual attention to each of our guests.

A typical day begins with supervised play in our doggie gym followed by nap time and a snack -- then it's time to play again! We recognize the individual needs of each dog and are happy to individualize his/her program to reflect appropriate activity levels.

All dogs must be free of internal and external parasites. Proof of appropriate immunization is required in order to provide all of our visitors with a healthy environment.

Doggie Daycare is open for daycare clients from Monday 7:30 am - 6:00 pm, Tuesday - Friday 6:30am - 6:00pm.
