Welcome to Fetch Inc.

Welcome To Fetch Inc., the canine daycare and education facility in Burnside, Dartmouth. We offer lots of experience in Doggie Daycare, Puppy Socialization and Training, Family Obedience, Agility and Rally-O. Along with daycare services we offer many types and levels of classes and events such as seminars, obedience, agility, run thrus, and fun matches for Conformation, Obedience and Agility. We also rent out space for training, matches, meetings and seminars.
