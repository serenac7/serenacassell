Fetch Inc

While I was between positions in 2018, I started working on this site to help the owner. I had noticed that the existing website was difficult to navigate and had very out-dated information.
I proposed making changes to the owner, and allowed them to set the pace for change.

The first challenge to overcome was finding a way to get the data from the existing site. The person who had maintained it was no longer available, so the files, images, and domain were not available.
The owner managed to register a new domain, and then transferred it over to me with direction so that I could keep it going and easily manage the site.

Once that was sorted, I had to keep this project somewhat on the back burner; the owner needed to take care of some things during the pandemic and didn't have time to review the website.

Website Mark 1.0

When I realized that the owner was having a hard time getting the volume of information, I scaled back the website to contain only what they needed.

Once that was finished and updates that were needed to update the prices and class descriptions were completed, it took less than a week to get sign off and bring the site live.
